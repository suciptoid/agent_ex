## Design Context

### Users
Mixed audience across technical and non-technical users in team/workspace contexts. The auth flow must be immediately understandable for first-time users while still feeling efficient for frequent users.

### Brand Personality
Calm, compact, simple. The interface should feel reliable and clear, reducing cognitive load and visual noise.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dual-theme (light and dark) support with a restrained product UI aesthetic. Prioritize clean surfaces, concise copy, and subtle micro-interactions over decorative effects.

### Design Principles
1. Keep hierarchy obvious with minimal visual elements.
2. Use compact spacing and concise labels for faster scanning.
3. Preserve comfort with soft contrast and gentle motion.
4. Maintain consistency across login, signup, and password reset screens.
5. Favor clarity and task completion over ornamental styling.
